The best food ever

  1. Sushi
    • Whats sushi?: Sushi is a popular Japanese dish that typically consists of vinegared rice (sushi rice) topped with various ingredients, most commonly seafood (often raw but can be cooked), but also vegetables. The sushi rice is seasoned with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, giving it a tangy and sweet flavor. Â 

      • Nigiri: Hand-pressed rice topped with a slice of raw fish or seafood.



      • Maki: Sushi rolls, typically made by rolling seaweed (nori) around a filling of rice and other ingredients.


      • Sashimi: Thin slices of raw fish or seafood served without rice.



      • Uramaki: Inside-out rolls, where the rice is on the outside and the seaweed is on the inside.



      • Temaki: Hand-rolled cones of seaweed filled with rice and other ingredients.

  1. Pizza

    • Whats pizza?: is a popular dish consisting of a flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. The base can be made from different types of dough, including wheat, whole grain, or gluten-free. The toppings can include meat, vegetables, cheese, and other ingredients.

      • Neapolitan pizza: A thin-crust pizza with a soft, chewy texture and a tangy tomato sauce.

      • New York-style pizza: A large, thin-crust pizza with a crispy edge and a tangy tomato sauce.



      • Chicago-style deep-dish pizza: A thick-crust pizza with a high edge and a chunky tomato sauce.


      • Sicilian-style pizza: A rectangular-shaped pizza with a thick crust and a sweet tomato sauce.
